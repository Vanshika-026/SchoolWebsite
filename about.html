GIRL'S EDUCATION

Many poor children need equal access to education. A child's future and general health are affected when they are unable to acquire quality education. A child's risk of being deceived by antisocial influences and becoming a threat to society is high.

Some of the top schemes are given below;

  1. Beti Bachao Beti Padhao.
  2. Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana.
  3. Balika Samridhi Yojana.
  4. CBSE Udaan Scheme.
  5. National Scheme of Incentives to Girls for Secondary Education.
